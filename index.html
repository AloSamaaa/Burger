About bryl's Burger

We are a burger restaurant based in Bonifacio High Street, Taguig which offers affordable yet satisfying, juicy, and lip-smacking burgers. It’s just beef, cheese and onions; no sauce or any other veggies. The steam from the juices of the beef and onions is locked into the buns. It’s just pure, in a burger sense. We're open Tuesdays to Sundays from 1:00 PM to 2:00 AM.
